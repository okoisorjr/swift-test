Exam Instructions

  1. Make sure that the picture and the exam number on the computer screen are yours. if they are not yours report to the supervisor.
  2. The examination consists of 100 OBJECTIVE questions and you are expected to answer all questions.
  3. The duration of the examination is 1 hour (60 minutes) and you can attempt the questions in any order you wish.
  4. If you are not familiar with the use of a computer mouse, use the A,B,C,D buttons on the keyboard to select an answer and use the N & P buttons on the keyboard to navigate to Next and Previous question
  5. If you are familiar with the mouse you can select, answers to the next questions by clicking on the option, and you can move to any question by clicking the question number at the bottom of the page.
  6. You are free to skip a question and come back to it later.
  7. Once a candidate submits his/her exam, it is not possible to go back or undo the submission.
  8. If your time is up, the examination will submit itself and you will be logged out.
  9. Click the Start Exam Button to begin your examination.
